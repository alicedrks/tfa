FYT – 2024

Find your type is a presentation website created as part of the Rethinking User Experience workshop .

The aim was to design a device, object or interface using Design Thinking methods . Find your type is a collaborative project in which we designed posters featuring different typographies . The purpose was to inspire the students while embellishing the class . During this project, I was mainly responsible for designing the posters .
